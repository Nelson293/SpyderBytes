Why choose a game-optimized PC build?

Do different games really need different PC builds?

Yes! Different games have different hardware requirements and performance characteristics. For example, competitive FPS games like Fortnite benefit from high refresh rates and strong single-core CPU performance, while open-world games like Cyberpunk 2077 demand powerful GPUs with lots of VRAM and multi-core CPUs.

What if I play multiple different games?

We can create a balanced build that delivers great performance across all your favorite games. Let us know which titles you play most frequently, and we'll design a custom PC that handles them all well, with priority given to your most-played games.

How do you determine the optimal build for each game?

We analyze each game's specific hardware requirements, benchmark data, and real-world performance metrics. We also consider your desired resolution, frame rate targets, and graphics settings to create a PC that delivers the best possible gaming experience for your budget.
