Your custom PC build questions answered

Can I buy the parts, and then you build my PC?

Of course, you can. If you want to do your research into compatibility and then buy all the components yourself, that's fine. You can then drop it all off at our HQ in 24 High St, Bagshot, GU19 5AA, for an assembly.

Do you build gaming PCs for kids?

We build custom PC gaming systems for kids, teenagers and adults.

How long does it take for you to build my PC?

Once we have all the parts, it takes on average 48 hours to build and assemble your gaming PC.

Do you offer prebuilt PCs for different budgets?

Yes, as well as offering built to spec computers, we also stock a number of pre-built and ready to buy computers that you can collect same day. Contact us and ask for more details.

How do I pay for my custom build PC?

If you are buying all the parts and then dropping them off to build you your dream gaming PC, you'll pay on the day you pick up your PC. We accept card payments too! If you want us to buy all the parts, we will send you a quote with the total price and pay by bank transfer.
