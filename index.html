Your custom PC build questions answered

Can I buy the parts, and then you build my PC?

Absolutely! You're welcome to research component compatibility and purchase all the parts yourself. Once you have everything, simply bring all components to our headquarters and we'll handle the assembly for you.

Do you build gaming PCs for kids?

We build custom PC gaming systems for kids, teenagers and adults.

How long does it take for you to build my PC?

After receiving all components, we typically complete the build and assembly of your gaming PC within 48 hours.

Do you offer prebuilt PCs for different budgets?

We not only offer custom-built computers to your specifications, but we also maintain a selection of pre-built systems ready for same-day collection. If you're interested in our in-stock options, please contact us for more information and availability.

What payment methods are available for my custom PC build?

If you're purchasing all the components yourself and bringing them to us for assembly into your dream gaming PC, payment will be due when you collect your completed system. We accept credit and debit cards for your convenience. Alternatively, if you'd prefer us to source all the parts for you, we'll provide a comprehensive quote with the total cost, and you can make payment via bank transfer.
